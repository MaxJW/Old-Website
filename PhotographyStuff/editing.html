Editing

Photoshop

Photoshop is the most commonly used photo editing tool industry wide, and contains a multitude of both beginner and advanced options for photos of all types.

Adobe Lightroom

Adobe Lightroom makes editing, uploading and sharing photos super easy! Highly reccomended for beginners!

GIMP

GIMP is an open-source, customisable and easy to use photosuite on par with paid software like Photoshop!

Paint.NET

Paint.NET is a free image editing program for Microsoft Windows, although lacking the features of Photoshop or GIMP it is extensive and easy to pick up for beginning and intermediate users.
